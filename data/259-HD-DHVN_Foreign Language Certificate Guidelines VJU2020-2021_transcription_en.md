# GUIDANCE

**On the use of foreign language certificates and certifications for confirming graduation requirements and organizing foreign language courses for full-time undergraduate students of the VJU2020 and VJU2021 cohorts at Vietnam-Japan University**

*Pursuant to Decision No. 1186/QĐ-TTg dated July 21, 2014 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of Vietnam-Japan University under Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Decision No. 391/QĐ-TTg dated March 31, 2017 of the Prime Minister on the Regulations on organization and operation of Vietnam-Japan University, Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Decision No. 5115/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated December 25, 2014 of the President of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on promulgating the Regulations on undergraduate training at Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Official Dispatch No. 4116/ĐHQGHN-ĐT dated December 14, 2021 of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on guidance for foreign language proficiency standards in undergraduate training;*

*Pursuant to Guidance No. 784/ĐHVN-ĐT dated October 9, 2020 of the Rector of Vietnam-Japan University on organizing foreign language courses in undergraduate training programs;*

*Pursuant to Official Dispatch No. 861/ĐHQGHN-ĐT dated March 14, 2024 of the President of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on credit recognition for foreign language courses for cohorts QH.2021 and earlier;*

Vietnam-Japan University provides the following guidance on using foreign language certificates and certifications for confirming graduation requirements and organizing foreign language courses for full-time undergraduate students of the VJU2020 and VJU2021 cohorts.

## 1. Applicable subjects

Full-time undergraduate students of the VJU2020 and VJU2021 cohorts.

## 2. Confirming graduation requirements

Before graduating, students shall submit a valid foreign language certificate or certification aligned with the Vietnamese Foreign Language Proficiency Framework and meeting the output standards of the training program (CTĐT), specifically:

| No. | Training Program | Vietnamese Foreign Language Proficiency Framework | |
| :-- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| | | **English** | **Japanese** |
| 1 | Japanese Studies | Level 3 | Level 4 |
| 2 | Computer Science and Engineering | Level 4 | – |

- See Appendix 1 of this Guidance for the list of certificates and certifications recognized at Vietnam National University, Hanoi.
*Note: Valid certificates and certifications are valid for 2 years from the exam date.*

## 3. Recognition of learning outcomes for foreign language courses within the training program framework

### 3.1. High-quality Computer Science and Engineering program

Students who submit a valid English certificate or certification shall receive recognition of learning outcomes for English B1 and English B2 courses in the General Knowledge Block (M1) as follows:

| No. | Vietnamese Foreign Language Proficiency Framework | Recognized Result | Course with Recognized Result | Course Code |
| :-- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Level 3 | Pass | English B1 | FLF1107 |
| 2 | Level 4 | Pass | English B1, English B2 | FLF1107, FLF1108 |

### 3.2. High-quality Japanese Studies program

**a) English**
- Recognition applies only to the English B1 course (FLF1107) in the disciplinary knowledge block and solely for the VNU-Tests certificate (VNU Professional Communication Proficiency Assessment). Other foreign language certificates are not accepted.
- Recognition based on VNU-Tests certificates is as follows:

| No. | Test Format | Recognized Score |
| :-- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | English Level 3 | 9.0 |
| 2 | English Level 4 | 10.0 |

**b) Japanese**
- Students shall follow Clause 3.1, Article 3 of Guidance No. 784/ĐHVN-ĐT dated October 9, 2020 on organizing foreign language courses in undergraduate training programs regarding Japanese B1 and Japanese B2 courses in the General Knowledge Block (M1).
- Recognized certificates and certifications are listed in Appendix 1 of this Guidance.

## 4. Important information to note

- Foreign language certificates and certifications must be valid at the time of submission. Students shall submit one notarized copy to the Department of Training and Student Affairs (Room 501, My Dinh Campus). Students who took the JLPT shall also submit the Certificate Appraisal Authorization Form according to the form in Appendix 2.
- Students already named in a Rector's decision recognizing fulfillment of graduation requirements are NOT required to resubmit their certificates or certifications.
- Foreign language certificates or certifications obtained via online examinations are not recognized at Vietnam-Japan University.
- Vietnam-Japan University encourages students to submit their certificates and certifications early to ensure timely verification before the graduation review period.
- For any questions regarding the submission of certificates, students should contact the Department of Training and Student Affairs directly (email: phongdaotao@st.vju.ac.vn).

Sincerely notified./.
