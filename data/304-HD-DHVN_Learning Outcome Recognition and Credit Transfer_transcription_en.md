# GUIDANCE

**On the recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer for students of undergraduate programs at Vietnam-Japan University**

*Pursuant to Decision No. 1186/QĐ-TTg dated July 21, 2014 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of Vietnam-Japan University under Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Decision No. 391/QĐ-TTg dated March 31, 2017 of the Prime Minister on the Regulations on organization and operation of Vietnam-Japan University, Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Decision No. 5115/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated December 25, 2014 of the President of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on promulgating the Regulations on undergraduate training at Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

*Pursuant to Decision No. 3626/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated October 21, 2022 of the President of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on promulgating the Regulations on undergraduate training at Vietnam National University, Hanoi;*

Vietnam-Japan University (VJU) hereby provides guidance to students on the recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer for students of undergraduate programs (CTĐT) at VJU as follows:

## I. Scope of application

Students enrolled in undergraduate programs at VJU.

## II. Principles for recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer

1. Courses previously completed by students shall be considered for recognition and credit transfer on the basis of comparing learning outcomes, course content and workload, assessment methods, and quality assurance conditions.
2. Foreign language certificates and certifications recognized by VJU (as officially published in the University's annual notices) shall be converted into credits for foreign language courses in the training program.
3. A student's scientific research output may be converted to replace an eligible elective course within the disciplinary knowledge block. Such conversion shall only apply when the research work has been evaluated and graded on a 10-point scale by the Academic Council, ensuring that it meets quality and applicability criteria based on comparison with the content of the corresponding course and the learning outcomes of the training program. For matters related to student scientific research achievements, refer to Article 29 of the Undergraduate Training Regulations 5115 or Article 32 of the Undergraduate Training Regulations 3626 at Vietnam National University, Hanoi.
4. For students changing majors, specialization tracks, training programs, or pursuing double-degree study: the Department of Training and Student Affairs (ĐT&CTSV) shall process the recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer in accordance with the approved List of Equivalent Courses.
5. Credit recognition and transfer limits:
- The total recognized credit load shall not exceed 50% of the minimum credit requirements of the training program.
- In student exchange programs, credits accumulated at institutions outside Vietnam National University, Hanoi shall not exceed 25% of the total credits of the training program.

## III. Procedures for recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer

1. Students shall submit an application for recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer, accompanied by supporting documents (notarized copies of transcripts, foreign language certificates/certifications).
2. The Academic Council of VJU shall review the results for recognition of learning outcomes and credit transfer for the courses accumulated by students into the training program on the basis of the principles set forth in this Guidance, finalize and forward the approved dossier to the Department of Training and Student Affairs.
3. Upon approval by the Rectorate Board, the Department of Training and Student Affairs shall enter the converted scores for students into VJU's academic management system.

## IV. Implementation

This Guidance shall take effect from the date of signing. In the course of implementation, should any issues arise, units and individuals shall report to the Rectorate Board (via the Department of Training and Student Affairs) for timely resolution.
