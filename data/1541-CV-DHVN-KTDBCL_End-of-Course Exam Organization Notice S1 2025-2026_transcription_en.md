**2.** Deadlines for submission of component grade sheets are as follows:

**2.2.** Hard copies signed in blue ink by the lecturer in charge of the course section shall be submitted to the Department of Training and Student Affairs, Room 105, Administration Building, Vietnam Japan University at Hoa Lac, no later than **3 days** from the last day of instruction for the course.

**3.** Deadlines for submission of the list of students ineligible to sit end-of-course examinations (with stated reasons) are as follows:

**3.1.** Soft copies (including a scanned grade sheet signed by the lecturer in blue ink, and a corresponding Excel file for cross-checking) shall be submitted no later than **1 day** from the last day of instruction for the course (pursuant to Article 38, Undergraduate Training Regulations) via email: vju-qa@vju.ac.vn

**3.2.** Hard copies signed in blue ink by the lecturer in charge of the course section shall be submitted to the QA Department, Room 204, Administration Building, Vietnam Japan University at Hoa Lac, no later than **3 days** from the last day of instruction for the course.

**Note:**

In the event of data errors attributable to the lecturer, the lecturer shall be responsible for submitting an explanation letter clearly stating the reason (with confirmation from the unit coordinating instruction for the course) and providing corrected data immediately upon discovery of the error.

Component grade sheets and lists of students ineligible to sit end-of-course examinations (hard copies signed by the lecturer in charge of the course) are mandatory archival records under the Undergraduate Training Regulations.

**4.** Lecturers, faculty leaders, directors, managers of undergraduate programs, and functional departments are responsible for implementing end-of-course examination procedures in strict accordance with Guidelines No. 1274/HD-QA dated October 27, 2025 of Vietnam Japan University on the organization of end-of-course examinations for Semester I, Academic Year 2025–2026.
