# Chapter I
GENERAL PROVISIONS

## Article 1. Scope of Application

This document regulates the management and attraction of foreigners studying in undergraduate, master's, doctoral training programs, short-term training and refresher programs, internships, student exchange, traineeships, and cultural exchange at Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU), hereinafter collectively referred to as international students.

## Article 2. Subjects of Application

- 1. These regulations apply to international students studying at VNU and related organizations and individuals.
- 2. These regulations apply to training units and service units responsible for the management, attraction, and support of international students at VNU.

## Article 3. Definitions

In these regulations, the following terms are understood as follows:

- 1. International students: Include agreement-based international students and non-agreement international students participating in in-person, online, or blended in-person and online learning.

---

- 2. Agreement-based international students: Foreigners admitted to study in Vietnam who are granted scholarships by the Vietnamese Government under international treaties to which Vietnam is a member.
- 3. Non-agreement international students: Foreigners, or Vietnamese nationals holding citizenship and residing abroad, who are sponsored by organizations or individuals for scholarships, or who self-fund their studies, research, or internships at VNU under agreements or contracts between training units and individual international students or with organizations and individuals sponsoring scholarships for international students, and who are not covered by the subjects specified in Clause 2 of this Article.
- 4. Training units: Units responsible for admitting, managing, and training international students at VNU, including member universities, member research institutes, affiliated schools, faculties, affiliated research institutes, and centers and units authorized to conduct research, training, and refresher courses in accordance with their functions and duties as prescribed by VNU and in compliance with legal regulations.
- 5. Attraction of international students: Strategies, policies, plans, and action programs of VNU and training units regarding admissions, training, scholarships, and provision of support services aimed at attracting international students to conduct research, study, intern, and participate in cultural exchange at VNU.
- 6. Long-term training programs: Training programs at the undergraduate, master's, and doctoral levels authorized for implementation at VNU, designed to provide learners with knowledge and skills in their field and specialization of study, and upon graduation, learners are awarded degrees corresponding to their level of training.
- 7. Short-term training programs: Training and refresher programs that may or may not award certificates or certifications (according to the functions of the training unit) delivered in-person, online, or in a blended format, designed to provide learners with professional knowledge and skills within a specified period as defined in each training program (under 01 year).
- 8. Student exchange and traineeship programs: Programs that admit international students for exchange study in-person, online, or in a blended format, with or without credit accumulation, participating in practical

---

training, fieldwork, scientific research, and cultural exchange activities at VNU under agreements signed by VNU or training units, within the framework of university associations, organizations of which VNU or training units are members, or under other agreements between VNU or training units and related organizations and individuals.

- 9. Service units: Units authorized to receive international students for accommodation and/or to carry out support activities for international students during their research, study, internship, and cultural exchange at VNU, including the Student Support Center and other relevant units within VNU assigned to serve international students as prescribed.
- 10. Partners: University associations, educational organizations, and higher education institutions with which VNU or training units have signed cooperation agreements or are members participating in training, refresher, exchange, research, and internship programs.
- 11. Scholarships: Funding from various sources that international students coming to VNU for research, study, internship, or exchange receive to fully cover or partially support tuition and living expenses during their time at VNU.

## Article 4. Objectives

- 1. To manage and attract international students to conduct research, study, intern, and participate in cultural exchange at VNU, contributing to integration efforts, internationalization of training programs, and increasing the international student index of VNU; to diversify training programs taught in foreign languages; to promote the foreign language teaching capacity of staff and lecturers; and to create opportunities for VNU students to study, interact, share experiences, and develop learning skills in an internationalized educational environment.
- 2. To contribute to promoting, developing the brand, and enhancing the standing of VNU and its training units on the international stage.

## Article 5. Principles of Implementation

- 1. To comply with the guidelines, policies, and regulations of the Party, the law, and State regulations on the management of foreigners studying in Vietnam.

---

- 2. To comply with the regulations and rules on admissions, organization, and management of training for long-term and short-term training programs, student exchange, research, internships, and cultural exchange as prescribed by VNU and under agreements signed between VNU/training units and partners, ensuring the rights of learners and related parties.

### Article 6. Languages Used in Teaching and Learning

- 1. Vietnamese is the official language used in training programs at VNU.
- 2. International students may conduct research, study, intern, and participate in cultural exchange in other languages that training units are authorized to use in training and research.

## Chapter II
ACTIVITIES FOR ATTRACTING INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS AT
VIETNAM NATIONAL UNIVERSITY, HANOI

### Article 7. Signing of Cooperation Agreements

- 1. VNU signs cooperation agreements with partners or participates as a member of university associations, organizations, and research, training, and student exchange projects.
- 2. Based on their capacity, conditions, and development orientation, training units sign cooperation agreements with partners according to the following delegation of authority:
   - a) For training units that are member universities and member research institutes: The unit signs cooperation agreements on research, training, student exchange, traineeships, and practical training with partners in accordance with its authority and current regulations of VNU. The content of signed agreements must be feasible and comply with State and VNU regulations. For agreements signed with foreign partners that involve coordination among training units within VNU, the lead training unit must consult with related units to reach consensus before signing;

---

   - b) For training units that are affiliated schools, faculties, institutes, and units authorized to conduct research, training, and refresher courses in accordance with their functions and duties: The unit negotiates cooperation activities with partners, drafts cooperation agreements, and reports to and requests VNU to sign the agreement or authorize the unit to sign with the partner.

### Article 8. Training Programs Applicable to International Students

- 1. Long-term training programs

Training units manage, organize training, and award degrees within their authority to international students enrolled in undergraduate, master's, and doctoral programs that have been issued by VNU, assigned for training, or licensed for implementation, in compliance with corresponding training regulations and relevant current provisions.

- 2. Short-term training programs

Training units develop and organize short-term training and refresher programs to meet the needs of international students and in accordance with agreements with partners (consistent with the fields in which the unit is authorized to provide training), with or without the awarding of certificates or certifications to learners. The teaching content in short-term training programs may be newly designed or adapted from existing course modules within training programs. The language of instruction shall be consistent with the organizational capacity of the training unit and with the agreements reached with partners and learners.

- 3. Student exchange and traineeship programs
   - a) Training units organize instruction of course modules within established training programs or develop new course modules, exchange programs, and research in Vietnamese or foreign languages (consistent with the unit's capacity and authorized fields of training) for incoming exchange international students;
   - b) Traineeship exchange programs may be implemented as a combination of research, study, teaching assistance, and practical training at training units in accordance with agreements between VNU or training units and partners, and in compliance with current regulations;
   - c) Student exchange programs may take the form of non-credit or credit-bearing study from 1 semester to 1 academic year (minimum credits to be accumulated: 06 credits/semester or 03 course modules), conducted in-person, online, or in a blended

---

in-person and online format; combining study with internships or cultural exchange. Student exchange programs for cultural exchange are developed and organized separately in accordance with agreements between VNU or training units and partners, and in compliance with current regulations.

### Article 9. Policies on Scholarships, Accommodation Support, and Services for International Students

- 1. Based on specific conditions, VNU and training units develop or strengthen the attraction of scholarship sources for international students. Scholarship schemes are developed at various levels and in different forms corresponding to specific selection criteria.
- 2. Depending on the type of international students coming for exchange under VNU's agreements with partners, VNU has policies for exempting or reducing accommodation fees for international students.
- 3. Training units may develop policies to support accommodation costs appropriate to the specific conditions of the unit, applicable to international students during their period of study, exchange, or internship at the unit; publicly announce information about accommodation support programs for international students on the unit's website and directly notify international students.
- 4. VNU, through the Student Support Center and other units assigned by VNU, provides accommodation services and accompanying services at appropriate costs to ensure learning and living conditions for international students during their time at VNU. VNU prioritizes providing accommodation services to first-year long-term international students at VNU.

### Article 10. Activities Supporting Research, Study, Internship, and Cultural Exchange for International Students

- 1. Training units are responsible for assigning and arranging teaching assistants and study mentors for international students at the unit; strengthening seminars, workshops, and academic forums to attract international students to conduct research, study, intern, and exchange at the unit.

---

- 2. In conjunction with training programs, training units strengthen the development and implementation of cultural exchange activities, student clubs, internship and fieldwork activities to attract international students to study, exchange, and conduct research at the unit.

### Article 11. Promotion of Information on Programs and Policies for Attracting International Students

- 1. VNU serves as the focal point for organizing and directing training units to jointly participate in admissions promotion programs and international education fairs appropriate to the needs and implementation capacity of training units; implements communication programs to attract international students to VNU and strengthens the posting of information on international student attraction programs of units on VNU's website.
- 2. Training units regularly update information in English and/or other foreign languages (if applicable) about the unit's training activities, training programs, admissions information, student life, scholarships, and policies for attracting international students on the unit's website, and simultaneously request VNU to post information on VNU's website to help international students easily learn about and contact the unit when they have needs for study or exchange at the training unit.
- 3. Training units proactively develop and implement programs to attract and recruit international students for study, exchange, and internship appropriate to the unit, publicly announced on the unit's website, VNU's website, and through appropriate media and information channels, and report to VNU as prescribed.

## Chapter III
ADMISSIONS, TRAINING, AND MANAGEMENT OF INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN LONG-TERM TRAINING PROGRAMS

### Article 12. Conditions for Application

- 1. Possess sufficient academic qualifications, competence, and expertise meeting the study and research requirements of the training program at each level:

---

   - a) For the undergraduate level: Graduated from upper secondary school or equivalent (recognized by Vietnam), meeting the admissions requirements of VNU and the training unit for the corresponding admissions year for the program applied to;
   - b) For the master's level: Graduated from university (or equivalent level or above) in a relevant field (for research-oriented master's programs, a graduation ranking of Good or above is required, or the applicant must have scientific publications related to the field of study and research) and meeting other admissions requirements of the training unit for the program applied to;
   - c) For the doctoral level: Graduated with a master's degree, or graduated from a full-time undergraduate program with a ranking of Distinction or above in a relevant field/specialization, or graduated at a level equivalent to Level 7 of the Vietnam National Qualifications Framework in certain specialized fields corresponding to the doctoral field/specialization, meeting conditions on research experience, scientific publications, research proposal, recommendation letters, and other admissions requirements of the training unit for the program applied to;
   - d) Supplementary coursework for international students applying for master's or doctoral programs shall be decided by the training unit in accordance with the admissions requirements of the program applied to, ensuring compliance with current VNU regulations on master's and doctoral training, and within a maximum of 01 year after the international student completes the preparatory language course;
   - đ) Training units are responsible for verifying the degrees, certificates, and other related documents (if any) of international students.
- 2. Possess sufficient proficiency in Vietnamese or a foreign language (if enrolling in a training program conducted in a foreign language) meeting the study and research requirements of the training program applied to, specifically as follows:
   - a) For the undergraduate level:

- International students applying for training programs conducted in Vietnamese must meet one of the following requirements:

---

+ Hold a Vietnamese language certificate equivalent to Level 4 or above according to the Vietnamese Language Proficiency Framework for Foreigners, issued by institutions authorized by the Ministry of Education and Training.

+ Hold a university degree in the field/specialization of Vietnamese language.

+ Hold a diploma from upper secondary school/vocational secondary school/college/university/master's/doctoral level where the language of instruction was Vietnamese.

- International students applying for training programs conducted in a foreign language: Must meet the requirements for a second foreign language as prescribed by the training program (if any) and one of the following requirements:

+ Be a citizen of a country whose official language is the foreign language used in the training program applied to.

+ Hold a foreign language certificate at Level 4 or above according to the 6-Level Foreign Language Proficiency Framework for Vietnam, issued by an institution authorized by the Ministry of Education and Training, or other equivalent foreign language certificates.

+ Hold a diploma from upper secondary school/vocational secondary school/college/university/master's/doctoral level where the language of instruction corresponds to the language used in the training program applied to.

   - b) For master's and doctoral programs:

- International students applying for master's programs conducted in Vietnamese must achieve Vietnamese proficiency at Level 4 or above according to the Vietnamese Language Proficiency Framework for Foreigners, or have graduated from university or equivalent level or above where the training program was taught in Vietnamese; and meet the requirements for a second foreign language as prescribed by the training program (if any).

- International students applying for master's or doctoral programs conducted in a foreign language: Must meet corresponding foreign language requirements, specifically by holding one of the following degrees or certificates:

---

+ A university degree or above in a foreign language field; or a university degree or above where the program was conducted primarily in a foreign language;

+ One of the degrees or certificates in the language of instruction at a level equivalent to Level 4 according to the 6-Level Foreign Language Proficiency Framework for Vietnam, or other equivalent certificates as announced by VNU, still valid as of the date of application (except for native speakers of the language used in the training program).

- 3. Be in sufficient health to study and conduct research in Vietnam. After arriving in Vietnam, international students must undergo a health examination at the VNU hospital or at medical facilities designated by the training unit. In cases of dangerous infectious diseases as prescribed by Vietnamese law or insufficient health for study, the student must return to their home country.
- 4. Age requirements for agreement-based international students shall be implemented in accordance with international treaties to which Vietnam is a member. There is no age restriction for non-agreement international students.
- 5. Have sufficient financial capacity to ensure study, research, internship, and living expenses in Vietnam for the corresponding duration;
- 6. Have a clear personal record, not be serving a criminal sentence, not be in violation of the laws of Vietnam or the country of origin; commit to entering Vietnam for the purpose of study.

Scientific publications may be articles in specialized scientific journals or scientific papers published in proceedings of national or international peer-reviewed conferences with ISBN publication numbers related to the field or research topic, recognized by the professorial title council of the discipline/interdisciplinary field; letter of recommendation from at least 01 scientist holding the title of professor or associate professor, or holding a Doctor of Science or doctoral degree;

Admission reviews shall be conducted a maximum of 3 times per year (corresponding to 3 academic terms per year);
   - c) Results notification: Based on the results of the Admission Council, if the international student meets the admission requirements for the training program as prescribed, the training unit shall notify the candidate, issue an admission decision, issue a recognition decision for the international student, or report to and request VNU to consider admission for the international student according to the following delegation of authority:

- For member universities and member research institutes: The head of the unit shall issue admission decisions and recognize students/learners/research fellows within their authority and report to VNU (through the Department of Academic Affairs).

- For affiliated schools, faculties, and member research institutes (assigned to provide master's-level training): The head of the unit shall report to and request VNU to issue admission decisions and recognition, or authorize the unit to issue admission decisions and recognize students/learners/research fellows for international students.

   - d) Dossiers requesting VNU to consider issuing decisions or authorizing affiliated schools, faculties, and member research institutes to issue admission and recognition decisions for international students shall include:

   - e) For dossiers of international students applying to affiliated schools, faculties, and member research institutes assigned to provide master's-level training, the Department of Academic Affairs shall coordinate, and the Department of Cooperation and Development shall serve as the focal point for dossier verification before submitting to the Board of Directors to issue decisions or authorize units to issue admission and recognition decisions for international students.

## Article 14. Preparatory Studies and Formal Program Studies

- 1. Preparatory studies
   - a) International students who have not yet attained sufficient Vietnamese language or foreign language proficiency (as the language of instruction in the program) to enter the formal program taught in Vietnamese or a foreign language that the training unit is authorized to use under programs issued by VNU shall be required to undertake preparatory Vietnamese or foreign language studies;
   - b) For agreement-based international students: The Ministry of Education and Training shall arrange for international students to study at Vietnamese language preparatory training institutions; the duration of preparatory Vietnamese studies shall be implemented in accordance with international treaties to which Vietnam is a party, agreements and accords signed between Vietnam and other countries, territories, and international organizations;

---

- c) For non-agreement international students: Training units shall organize preparatory Vietnamese or foreign language programs (as the language of instruction in the program) for international students, or send international students to educational institutions providing preparatory Vietnamese or foreign language training. The duration of preparatory training for non-agreement international students shall be implemented in accordance with the training program in which the international student participates, or in accordance with the contract or agreement between the training unit and the international student or the scholarship-sponsoring partner (if any);
   - d) Upon completion of the preparatory Vietnamese or foreign language course, international students must take a Vietnamese or foreign language proficiency examination; if they meet the Vietnamese or foreign language admission requirements as prescribed in Article 12 of these Regulations, they shall be transferred to the formal program; if they do not meet the requirements, they must continue supplementary studies and take another examination until they meet the requirements and are awarded a Vietnamese or foreign language certificate to be transferred to the formal program.
- 2. Formal program studies
   - a) International students who hold a Vietnamese language proficiency certificate meeting admission requirements as prescribed, or who have graduated from general education, secondary vocational education, college, undergraduate, master's, or doctoral levels in Vietnamese, and who meet the conditions regarding health, age, academic qualifications, professional expertise, and finances prescribed in Article 12 of these Regulations shall be considered for admission to the formal program taught in Vietnamese;
   - b) International students who meet the foreign language admission requirements (as the language of instruction of the training program), and the conditions regarding academic qualifications, professional expertise, health, age, and finances prescribed in Article 12 of these Regulations shall be considered for admission to the formal program taught in a foreign language.

### Article 15. Course Components Not Mandatory for International Students

- 1. International students shall be exempted from the foreign language graduation requirements prescribed for Vietnamese citizens studying corresponding training programs taught in Vietnamese.

During the training process, the head of the training unit shall consider organizing advanced Vietnamese language courses to replace foreign language courses for international students.

---

- 2. International students at the undergraduate level shall be exempted from the National Defense and Security Education course and may choose replacement courses including: Vietnamese Culture, Vietnamese History, or other elective courses as decided by the head of the training unit based on the actual conditions of the unit.
- 3. The provisions of Clause 2 of this Article shall not apply to cases where international students are admitted to specializations in national defense and security.

   - b) Agreement-based international students shall not unilaterally extend their study and research period; in cases where an extension is needed to complete the training program, including the preparatory Vietnamese language study period, the written consent of the sending party and the written approval of the head of the unit where the international student is studying shall be required;
   - c) For non-agreement international students, the extension of the study period shall be implemented in accordance with the agreement, training contract, and regulations of the training unit where the international student is studying, in compliance with the current training regulations of VNU.
- 3. Temporary suspension of studies
   - a) During the course of study, agreement-based international students may temporarily suspend their studies for a maximum of 12 months if there is a justified reason approved by the sending party and permitted in writing by the training unit;

---

- b) For non-agreement international students, the period of temporary suspension of studies shall be implemented in accordance with the agreement or training contract between the international student and the unit, and in compliance with the unit's regulations and the training regulations of VNU.

   - b) Transfer of training institution:

The international student must obtain the written consent of the receiving educational institution.

### Article 17. Training Expenses and Scholarships

- 1. For agreement-based international students
   - a) Standards, entitlements, and training expenses shall be implemented in accordance with the current regulations of the Ministry of Finance and international treaties to which Vietnam is a party;
   - b) International students who must extend their study period to complete the training program due to transfer of field of study, change of training institution, or personal failure to meet academic requirements resulting in inability to maintain the prescribed study progress shall not receive the scholarship entitlements currently being enjoyed during the extension period. All costs arising from the extension of the study period shall be borne by the sending party and/or the international student, except for cases specifically provided for in international treaties to which Vietnam is a party;
   - c) International students who temporarily suspend their studies shall not receive scholarship standards and entitlements during the suspension period. After the suspension period, if the international student meets the conditions and is permitted by the training unit to continue studying, they shall continue to receive standards and entitlements as prescribed. The total funded period shall not exceed the total study period stated in the original Decision.
- 2. For non-agreement international students
   - a) Training expenses shall be implemented in accordance with the agreement or training contract signed between the scholarship-sponsoring organization or individual and VNU, with the training unit, or in accordance with the contract between the individual international student and the training unit (ensuring no conflict with current State regulations). International students shall bear all other costs arising during the study period at VNU; tuition fees and other fees must be notified to international students prior to enrollment;
   - b) In cases where international students defer enrollment or extend the training period but not exceeding the maximum training duration of the program, retake courses, retake examinations, etc., they must pay fees as prescribed by the training unit, in compliance with the regulations of the competent state authority;

---

- c) International students who meet the scholarship criteria of the training unit may be considered for scholarships in accordance with the policies and scholarship programs of the training unit applicable to the corresponding training cohorts (if any).

Training units must fully and accurately update international student information (including international students enrolled in joint training programs with foreign partners and VNU training programs) in the electronic database system for international student management at http://lhsnn.vied.vn no later than 30 days after the international student arrives in Vietnam for enrollment, and update information annually or when there are changes.
- 2. Training units shall submit reports on the reception, training, and academic results of international students (following the template in the Appendix of Circular No. 30/2018/TT-BGDĐT dated December 24, 2018 of the Ministry of Education and Training) to the Ministry of Education and Training (through the Department of International Cooperation) before December 31 of each year (via postal mail and data files uploaded to the reporting section of the electronic database system for international student management at http://lhsnn.vied.vn).
- 3. Training units shall update the information of international students enrolled in long-term degree-granting training programs on VNU's training management software in accordance with the current training regulations.

## Chapter IV

**ADMISSION, TRAINING, AND MANAGEMENT OF INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN SHORT-TERM TRAINING PROGRAMS**

### Article 19. Admission

- 1. Admission: VNU and training units shall announce training programs, plans, and training schedules on the international student admission software, on the websites of VNU and training units, and through other appropriate media channels. Admission information shall include:

---

- Training program, mode and duration of training, language of instruction, training capacity, certificates and certifications to be awarded (if any).
- Tuition fees, scholarships (if any), and other related expenses during the program at the unit.
- 2. Admission dossier: Application for enrollment; evidence of Vietnamese or foreign language proficiency (if required by the program), passport, and other documents as required by the unit (if any).
- 3. Training units shall issue decisions to receive short-term international students at the unit within their authority and manage international student dossiers as prescribed.
- 2. Admission dossier: Application for enrollment; evidence of Vietnamese or foreign language proficiency (if required by the program), passport, and other documents as required by the unit (if any).
- 3. Training units shall issue decisions to receive short-term international students at the unit within their authority and manage international student dossiers as prescribed.

## **Article 20. Organization of Training**

- 1. Training units shall organize training in accordance with the program agreed upon and signed between the parties. Based on the number of international students and the characteristics of the training program, the unit shall arrange instructors and training schedules appropriately to ensure the progress and objectives of the program as agreed with the partner.
- 2. Training units shall be responsible for registering the information of international students studying short-term programs at training units on VNU's international student admission software (at http://admissions-apply.vnu.edu.vn/) no later than 30 days after the conclusion of the training program; managing international students during their participation in short-term training programs in accordance with current regulations; and coordinating with competent authorities to resolve matters related to international students during their study and residence in Vietnam.
- 3. Assessment of learning outcomes: Methods of examination and assessment of learners shall be clearly specified in the training program. Training units may conduct assessments for each course component or conduct an overall assessment at the end of the program in accordance with the methods published in the issued training program.

## **Article 21. Training Programs**

- 1. Short-term training programs shall be developed, issued, and implemented by training units to meet the needs of learners (in accordance with the fields in which the unit is authorized to provide training), with or without the issuance of certificates or certifications; training objectives of the program shall be in accordance with the agreement with the partner/learner, ensuring no conflict with Vietnamese

---

law and VNU regulations. The content of training and professional development programs shall not be detrimental to national defense, national security, or community interests; shall not propagate religion, distort history; and shall not adversely affect Vietnamese culture, morality, or fine customs and traditions. Programs in the fields of law, religion, security, ethnicity, human rights, and other specialized training fields as prescribed must obtain approval from VNU before implementation.

- 2. Training programs must clearly state the content, training objectives, language of instruction, mode of training, examination and assessment methods, and conditions for the issuance of certificates or certifications (if any).
- 3. Training programs shall be issued/approved by the head of the training unit before implementation. This shall serve as the basis for the unit to organize training and issue certificates or certifications to international students (if any).

## **Article 22. Knowledge Volume and Training Duration**

- 1. The knowledge volume of each short-term training program shall be allocated across one or more course components/modules, depending on the objectives and regulations of each program or in accordance with the agreement signed with the partner. The volume of each course component may include theoretical instruction and practical/self-study hours, or follow the content structure agreed upon with the partner and the learner. Training units shall approve the teaching content or issue course syllabi before instruction begins.
- 2. Training duration: The duration of training, professional development, and advancement shall be specified in each program, in accordance with the agreement with the partner and the learner, and shall not exceed 01 year.

## **Article 23. Issuance of Certificates and Certifications of Program Completion**

- 1. Certificate and certification templates shall be developed and approved by the unit, ensuring compliance with the current regulations on diploma, certificate, and certification management of VNU and of the relevant competent state authority (if any).
- 2. VNU or training units shall issue certificates or certifications to learners when the following conditions are met:

---

- Completion of the short-term training program meeting all requirements regarding duration, program content, and course content as agreed between the training unit and the sending partner or the individual international student, with assessment results meeting the program requirements.
- Fulfillment of obligations regarding tuition fees, charges, and related expenses as prescribed or as stipulated in the signed agreement.
- No violation of the law or provisions related to the issuance of certificates and certifications as prescribed by VNU and the relevant competent state authority (if any).
- 3. Timeline for issuance of certificates and certifications (if any): No later than 30 days from the date of conclusion of the training program.
- 3. Timeline for issuance of certificates and certifications (if any): No later than 30 days from the date of conclusion of the training program.

# **Chapter V**

# **RECEPTION, TRAINING, AND MANAGEMENT OF INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS PARTICIPATING IN STUDENT EXCHANGE AND INTERNSHIP PROGRAMS**

## **Article 24. Types of Exchange Programs, Program Levels, and Exchange Duration**

- 1. Cultural exchange program: An exchange program whose main activity is organizing cultural exchange and exploration of Vietnamese culture for international students, without credit accumulation.
- 2. Cultural exchange combined with study program: An exchange program combining Vietnamese cultural exchange with the study of certain course components, with or without credit accumulation. The partner's consideration of credit recognition within the program shall be based on the agreement between VNU or the training unit and the partner.
- 3. Credit-bearing exchange program: A program in which international students study certain course components and accumulate credits through in-person, online, or blended modes of instruction, taught by instructors of the training unit or jointly taught by instructors of the training unit and instructors of

---

partner universities; course components in this program shall belong to long-term training programs issued by VNU or be programs/course components specifically designed for exchange students developed by the training unit in agreement with the partner, with course syllabi in accordance with VNU regulations. Upon completion of the program, international students shall be issued transcripts or study confirmations, and credits shall be considered for recognition by the partner in accordance with the agreement.

- 4. International students participating in internship exchange programs may combine scientific research, teaching assistance, or participation in internships and fieldwork. Interns in specialized fields must complete the program content according to the internship syllabus within the deadline accepted by the training unit, and report internship and research results periodically as prescribed by the unit. Upon completion of the program, international students shall be issued a confirmation letter by the training unit.
- 5. Levels of exchange programs
   - a) VNU-level exchange program: Implemented in accordance with cooperation agreements between VNU and the partner. Based on the partner's request to send international students for exchange, VNU shall request the training unit to receive, organize training and internships, and manage the international students;
   - b) Training unit-level exchange program: Implemented in accordance with agreements signed by the unit with the partner or signed by VNU and assigned to the unit for implementation. The unit shall proactively implement the program and be responsible for receiving, organizing training and internships, and managing exchange international students, and reporting in accordance with current regulations.
- 6. Exchange duration
   - a) For cultural exchange and cultural exchange combined with study programs: International students shall participate in exchange programs for the duration specified in each program, in accordance with the agreement signed with the partner;
   - b) For credit-bearing exchange programs: International students may participate in exchange programs for 01 semester or 02 semesters (01 year) according to each program or in accordance with the agreement signed by VNU or the training unit with the partner.

---

## **Article 25. Selection Criteria and Exchange Quotas**

- 1. Selection criteria for international students participating in exchange and cultural exchange programs:
   - a) International students shall meet the requirements and conditions of the exchange program that VNU or the training unit has signed an agreement with the partner;
   - b) International students shall be nominated by the partner to participate in the exchange program.
- 2. Selection criteria for credit-bearing exchange programs
   - a) Being students/learners/research fellows from partner universities, associations, organizations, or international projects with which VNU or the training unit has signed cooperation agreements or of which it is a member, and nominated by the partner universities, associations, organizations, or projects for exchange at VNU;
   - b) Having completed a minimum of 02 semesters and achieved a GPA of 2.8/4.0 or above (cumulative GPA across completed semesters) or equivalent;
   - c) Foreign language or Vietnamese language proficiency requirements:

- For international students studying in a foreign language exchange program: Holding an English language certificate at Level 4 under the 6-Level Foreign Language Proficiency Framework for Vietnam, or IELTS 5.5 or above, or equivalent (for exchange programs taught in English), or meeting the B2 foreign language requirement under the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (for exchange programs taught in a foreign language other than English). Foreign language certificates must be valid as prescribed.
- Cases exempted from foreign language certificates include: International students from countries whose native language is the language used for study in the exchange program; international students participating in training programs in their home country taught in English or another foreign language that is the language of exchange study at VNU, with written confirmation from the partner affirming that the international student has sufficient corresponding foreign language proficiency for exchange study at VNU.
- For cases where international students study in Vietnamese, they must provide evidence of Vietnamese language proficiency at Level 4 under the Vietnamese Language Proficiency Framework for Foreigners

---

or equivalent. In cases where international students do not yet hold a Vietnamese language certificate but are participating in training programs taught in Vietnamese in their home country, the partner university must provide written confirmation affirming that the international student has sufficient Vietnamese language proficiency for exchange study at VNU, or the training unit shall coordinate with the University of Social Sciences and Humanities to assess Vietnamese language proficiency for exchange study before receiving the international student. Costs for Vietnamese language proficiency assessment (if any) shall be borne by the international student.

   - d) Passport with validity period sufficient for the duration of participation in exchange programs and in compliance with current Vietnamese regulations;
   - đ) Good health meeting the requirements for exchange study at VNU;
   - e) Commitment to study and fully comply with all relevant regulations on study and residence of VNU or the training unit and Vietnamese law throughout the exchange study period at VNU.

- 3. Training units may establish criteria appropriate to their unit-level exchange programs, provided they do not conflict with the criteria in Clause 2 of this Article. For cultural exchange programs, compliance shall be in accordance with the agreement with the partner for each specific program.
- 4. For exchange international students from partner universities that have not yet signed cooperation agreements with VNU or the training unit: The training unit shall consider and carry out the exchange reception in accordance with the agreement signed with the international student and in compliance with the criteria in Clause 2 of this Article.
- 5. Exchange quotas: Units shall independently determine the quota for each student exchange program in accordance with the unit's training capacity and/or the quota specified in the agreement signed by VNU or the unit with the partner, and report to VNU as prescribed.

## Article 26. Program Announcement

- 1. VNU shall announce student exchange programs under cooperation agreements that VNU has signed with partners or participates in as a member. Information on exchange programs shall be sent to member units and published on VNU's official website.

---

- 2. Training units shall proactively develop plans to attract international students for exchange and regularly update and publish on their unit's official website information including academic programs, conditions and quotas for accepting international students, funding and support available to international students, contact information, and other relevant information. Units may request VNU to support the publication of information on VNU's official website.

## Article 27. Selection and Admission Process for Incoming Exchange Students

- 1. For VNU-level exchange programs

   - a) Exchange students shall submit applications online through VNU's international student admissions software (at http://admissions-apply.vnu.edu.vn/), via email, or submit applications directly as instructed by the Cooperation and Development Department or the designated contact unit;
   - b) VNU shall receive exchange information from partners, coordinate with training units on the capacity to accept incoming exchange students; assign training units to review and evaluate applications, contact international students for examination and interviews (if necessary), and notify partners of admission results; guide international students through procedures for exchange study at VNU. In cases where VNU serves as the direct focal point responsible for reviewing and admitting international students as stipulated in regulations/agreements with partners, VNU (through the Selection Committee coordinated by the Cooperation and Development Department) shall carry out the selection and admission procedures and assign units to manage and organize training for international students during the exchange period;
   - c) Training units shall provide academic advising, issue acceptance letters, carry out admission procedures for exchange students, assist with visa application procedures, arrange suitable accommodation in VNU dormitories, and organize training, issue academic transcripts (mandatory for credit-bearing exchange programs), and manage incoming exchange students at the unit;
   - d) For cases involving the implementation of specialized exchange programs or projects, VNU shall review and decide.

---

- 2. For training unit-level exchange programs

   - a) International students shall submit applications online through VNU's international student admissions software (at http://admissions-apply.vnu.edu.vn/), via email, or submit directly to the address as instructed by the units;
   - b) Training units shall carry out application review, provide information advising, send acceptance letters and admit incoming exchange students, assist with visa applications as regulated, and organize training for international students at the unit.

## Article 28. Management, Training Organization, and Credit Recognition for Exchange Students

- 1. Training units shall manage international students during their study period at the unit in accordance with relevant current regulations; disseminate regulations on training, and on the rights and responsibilities of international students for their awareness and compliance.
- 2. Coordinate with the Student Support Center or relevant assigned units to arrange suitable accommodation for international students in accordance with room standards and support services approved by VNU under current regulations; inspect and manage the academic progress, training, and daily activities of international students.
- 3. Organize training for exchange students in compliance with VNU's current training regulations or in accordance with agreements signed by VNU or training units with partners, or in accordance with the regulations of associations or programs of which VNU or training units are members.
- 4. Assign management staff and Vietnamese students to assist and support international students during the exchange period at the unit.
- 5. Issue transcripts for students participating in credit-bearing exchange programs and/or certificates of exchange program completion (if applicable).
- 6. The recognition of exchange credits shall be decided by the partner sending the international students. Training units shall issue transcripts or confirm internships, fieldwork, and other relevant documents to support partners in recognizing credits for international students.

---

## Article 29. Funding for Exchange Program Participation

- 1. International students shall participate in exchange programs voluntarily, understand, and accept all related financial terms announced by the program.
- 2. Fees and tuition for exchange: International students shall be exempted from library fees and exchange tuition fees in accordance with agreements signed between VNU or training units and partners or organizations of which VNU or training units are members. Training units shall determine the fee and tuition levels for free-mover exchange students (where the unit has not signed an agreement with the student's home institution abroad).
- 3. Living allowances and other expenses: Living allowances and other expenses that incoming exchange students receive or are required to contribute shall depend on the terms signed between VNU or training units and partners, organizations, or individual international students under each exchange program and shall be clearly communicated to international students.
- 4. Accommodation fees

   - a) International students participating in credit-bearing exchange programs under cooperation agreements between VNU and partners or organizations of which VNU is a member shall receive accommodation support or fee exemptions in accordance with the regulations of the Student Support Center and the signed agreements. Accommodation fee exemptions may be adjusted or changed in accordance with VNU's regulations;
   - b) For international students participating in other exchange programs or projects: International students shall pay accommodation fees and other service charges in accordance with the regulations of each exchange program. The payment amount shall be as agreed between the international students and the training unit or service unit, in accordance with VNU's regulations.

- 5. Scholarships

   - a) VNU and training units shall, based on actual operations and current regulations, attract and establish scholarship funds to support exchange students. Scholarships include full or partial scholarships at various levels based on VNU's current scholarship regulations and the financial balance of VNU and training units;

---

   - b) VNU scholarships shall be reviewed and decided by VNU regarding the scholarship levels, number of scholarships, and scholarship award criteria (if any). Each incoming exchange student may be considered for scholarship support once;
   - c) Scholarships of training units shall be reviewed and decided by the units regarding the scholarship levels, number of scholarships, and scholarship award criteria (if any);
   - d) The exchange scholarship fund at VNU (if any) shall be sourced from various lawful revenue sources, which may be allocated from tuition revenue in accordance with current legislation and from donated or sponsored funds received by VNU or training units from social resources, organizations, and individuals. For other scholarships, implementation shall follow the regulations of the corresponding exchange programs or projects.

# Chapter VI

# RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

## Article 30. Rights of international students

- 1. To be admitted to study in the field for which the international student registered for admission; to be guaranteed the rights of VNU students; to be informed of training regulations and provided with complete information on studies at the training unit.
- 2. To receive counseling and support in finding accommodation or to be given priority for placement in VNU dormitories; to be fully informed of the regulations of international student service facilities within VNU; to use equipment and facilities serving academic, physical education, and sports activities of the training unit and international student service facilities within VNU.
- 3. To participate in cultural, artistic, physical education, and sports activities for students organized by the training unit and international student service facilities within VNU; to be facilitated in learning about Vietnamese customs, traditions, and culture.
- 4. To participate in scientific research activities, seminars, and academic symposia organized by VNU or the training unit.

---

- 5. To take examinations, assessments, defend theses, capstone projects, dissertations, and graduation dissertations, and to receive diplomas and diploma supplements, certificates, certifications, and transcripts (if any) corresponding to the training program in which the student participated.
- 6. International students enrolled in long-term degree-granting programs are entitled to return to their home country for summer holidays and public holidays, to invite relatives to visit in accordance with Vietnamese regulations; to take leave, sick leave, or medical leave with the consent of the training unit.
- 7. Agreement-based international students shall enjoy the standards and benefits in accordance with current Vietnamese regulations and international treaties to which Vietnam is a party. Non-agreement international students may be considered by the unit for scholarships under the unit's programs (if any).
- 8. A group of international students from the same country, studying at VNU or residing in the same dormitory or international student service facility of VNU, may elect a representative to manage all aspects concerning international students of their country and to serve as a liaison with the training unit or international student service facility to resolve matters related to the group of international students from their country.
- 9. Exchange international students shall receive scholarships in accordance with agreements between VNU and partners and in accordance with the law.

### Article 31. Responsibilities of international students

- 1. To comply with Vietnamese law and respect Vietnamese customs and traditions.
- 2. To comply with the Regulations on Management of Foreigners Studying in Vietnam, the University Charter issued by the Ministry of Education and Training of Vietnam, the training regulations corresponding to the training level of VNU, and the rules, academic regulations, exchange regulations, and living regulations established by the training unit and service facilities.

- 3. Comply strictly with regulations and the stated purpose of entry into Vietnam for study. Abide by the regulations on residence, exit, and entry for foreigners in accordance with the current regulations of Vietnam.
- 4. Maintain friendly relations with Vietnamese citizens and international students from other countries.
- 5. Preserve and protect the property of the training institution and the facilities serving international students.

---

- 6. Submit complete documentation as required, complete procedures to participate in personal insurance, health insurance, register for residence, and comply with residence regulations in accordance with the current regulations of Vietnam; fulfill all financial obligations fully and promptly in accordance with the regulations of the training institution.
- 7. When going on excursions or traveling during holidays or Tet breaks, international students must report to the training institution or the facility serving international students. In such cases, international students must handle all procedures and expenses themselves and bear full responsibility for their own safety, health, and other matters.

# Chapter VIII
COMMENDATION, HANDLING OF VIOLATIONS, AND ORGANIZATION OF IMPLEMENTATION

## Article 34. Commendation and handling of violations regarding international students

- 1. International students with outstanding achievements in study, scientific research, and friendship activities shall be commended in accordance with the provisions of Vietnamese law, of VNU and the training unit regarding emulation and commendation.
- 2. International students who violate discipline shall, depending on the severity of the violation, be handled in the following forms:

   - a) Reprimand;
   - b) Warning;
   - c) Suspension from study and repatriation;
   - d) Referral to competent authorities for handling in accordance with the laws of Vietnam.

---

- 3. The disciplinary forms prescribed at Points a and b, Clause 2 of this Article shall be decided by the head of the training unit or the unit serving international students within their authority. The disciplinary forms prescribed at Points c and d, Clause 2 of this Article shall be decided by the head of the training unit or the unit serving international students in agreement with the sending party and the scholarship-granting party for non-Agreement international students; and shall be reported to VNU and to the Ministry of Education and Training for decision regarding Agreement international students.

## Article 35. Commendation and handling of violations regarding training units and units serving international students

- 1. Organizations and individuals with outstanding achievements in training, managing, and serving international students shall be commended in accordance with VNU's provisions on emulation and commendation.
- 2. Organizations and individuals who commit acts in violation of the provisions of this Regulation and related provisions of VNU and of the law shall, depending on the severity of the violation, be handled in accordance with the provisions of the law.

## Article 36. Reporting regime

- 1. Database on international students: Training units shall update information on international students coming to study and exchange at their units on VNU's foreign student admission software and the information portal of the Ministry of Education and Training, and report to the Ministry as prescribed in Article 18 of this Regulation, and concurrently send reports to VNU.
- 2. Before December 31 of each year, training units shall report on documents and cooperation agreements signed with partners, short-term training programs that have been issued and organized for international students, and admission work, training management, the number of international students enrolled, the number of degrees, certificates, and certifications issued, or submit ad hoc reports at the request of VNU or competent state agencies.
- 3. During the course of implementation, units shall promptly report to VNU any unusual issues arising in relation to international students participating in long-term, short-term, exchange, and internship training programs at their units, or any difficulties requiring guidance. Annually, VNU shall organize inspections of the management and attraction of foreigners studying at VNU.

---

## Article 37. Implementation provisions

- 1. This Regulation shall take effect from April 1, 2024 and shall replace the "Regulation on Attraction and Management of International Students at VNU" issued together with Decision No. 4848/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated December 18, 2017, and shall replace the contents related to international students coming for exchange in the "Guidelines for Implementation of Student Exchange with Foreign Universities at Vietnam National University, Hanoi" issued together with Decision No. 3763/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated November 7, 2012.
- 2. Special cases in the management and attraction of international students shall be decided by the President of VNU. During the course of implementation, this Regulation may be adjusted, supplemented, and amended as appropriate. Adjustments shall be considered and decided by the President of VNU within their authority.
