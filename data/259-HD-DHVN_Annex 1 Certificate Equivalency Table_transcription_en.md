- *The scores listed above are the minimum scores that learners must achieve.*
- *Japanese certificates recognized for three skills (Listening – Reading – Writing) are JLPT, NAT-TEST, and J-TEST.*
- *For other less common international foreign language certificates or certifications, Vietnam-Japan University shall request the University of Languages and International Studies to confirm the equivalent score conversion.*
- *Vietnam-Japan University does not recognize foreign language certificates or certifications obtained via online examinations./.*
