1.  **Eligible applicants, scope, and admission requirements**

**1.1. Eligible applicants**

In accordance with the current admissions regulations of the Ministry of Education and Training (MOET) and Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU), eligible applicants include:

\+ Candidates who have graduated from a Vietnamese upper secondary education program (through formal education or continuing education) or who have graduated from a foreign upper secondary education program (authorized by the host country, equivalent to the Vietnamese upper secondary level) conducted abroad or in Vietnam;

\+ Candidates who have graduated at the intermediate level and have completed the required volume of upper secondary cultural knowledge as prescribed by the Education Law and its implementing guidelines.

**1.2. Admissions scope:** Vietnam Japan University recruits nationwide and internationally.

**1.3. Admission requirements**

\+ Must be in good health for study as required by current regulations. For persons with disabilities who have certification from competent authorities regarding deformity, disability, or reduced capacity for self-care in daily life and studies, the Admissions Council Chairperson shall consider and decide.

\+ Candidates must have complete personal information and application documents as required.

\+ Foreign candidates must satisfy the regulations on management and attraction of foreigners studying at VNU under Decision No. 5292/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated December 29, 2023 of the Director of VNU; and the regulations on management of foreigners studying in Vietnam under Circular No. 30/2018/TT-BGDĐT dated December 24, 2018 of MOET.

**2.4. Method based on foreign language certificates combined with 2026 Upper Secondary Graduation Examination scores**

– Candidates may use English or Japanese language certificates to register for admission in accordance with VNU's regular undergraduate admissions regulations, with language certificates as specified in Appendix I.

– Score scale: 30-point scale. Comprising the converted score from the certified language certificate results and the 2 remaining subjects in the subject combination corresponding to the registered training program.

**2.5. Method based on SAT results** (Scholastic Assessment Test, United States)

– Candidates may use SAT results for admission in accordance with the 2026 Admissions Regulations of MOET and VNU.

VNU's registration code with the SAT administering organization (The College Board) is **7853-Vietnam National University-Hanoi** (*candidates must declare this registration code when registering for the SAT*).

**2.6. Interview and candidate assessment method**

***2.6.1. Admission requirements:***

For candidates who graduated from upper secondary school before 2026:

- Must hold an upper secondary graduation diploma;
- Must have academic performance and conduct classifications of Good or higher for all 3 years of upper secondary school;
- Must have a combined average score for the 3 years of upper secondary school in 02 subjects: Mathematics and Literature of 15 points or higher.

For candidates who graduated from upper secondary school abroad:

- Must hold an upper secondary graduation diploma;
- Must have a cumulative grade point average (GPA) of 2.5 or higher, or equivalent;
- Must have a clear and lawful personal record as prescribed by law.

***2.6.2. Examination and interview:***

Candidates undergo an interview and write an essay or take a multiple-choice test according to each training program as specified below:

3.  **Rules for equivalent conversion of admission thresholds and passing scores between subject combinations and admission methods.**

**3.1. Equivalent conversion rules.**

- ***Selection based on 2026 High School Graduation Exam results.***

Converted score (ĐQĐ) =  + Priority points (ĐT, KV)

*[Note: The source page omits the term before "+" in the converted score (ĐQĐ) formula. Điểm ƯT (ĐT, KV) = priority points by category/object and region.]*

*(Common subjects detailed in section 3.2.2)*

- ***Selection based on Competency Assessment Test (HSA) results for high school students organized by VNU in 2026.***

– Conversion follows the scoring scale of the Institute of Digital Training & Testing (VNU)

– Candidates can visit the following link to look up the percentile and score ranking of the HSA 2026 test: [https://quydoi.hsa.edu.vn](https://quydoi.hsa.edu.vn/) or [https://quydoi-hsa.vnu.edu.vn](https://quydoi-hsa.vnu.edu.vn/).

- ***Selection based on foreign language certificates combined with 2026 High School Graduation Exam scores***

Converted score (ĐQĐ) =  + Priority points (ĐT, KV)

*[Note: The source page omits the term before "+" in the converted score (ĐQĐ) formula. Điểm ƯT (ĐT, KV) = priority points by category/object and region.]*

*(Common subjects detailed in section 3.2.2)*

- ***Selection based on standardized SAT results*** *(Scholastic Assessment Test, USA)*

– Principle: Convert SAT results to a 30-point scale to determine admission thresholds and passing scores. Specific information will be announced after the 2026 High School Graduation Exam results are available. Refer to the 2025 conversion scores.

**3.2. Admission thresholds**

1.  For foreign candidates enrolled in Vietnamese-taught programs, a Vietnamese language certificate at Level 4 or above under the 6-level foreign language proficiency framework for foreigners, issued by institutions authorized by MOET, is required. This does not apply to foreign candidates applying for the Global Development and Innovation program, as it is an English-taught program.
2.  For candidates participating in the "Semiconductor Industry Human Resource Development Program to 2030, with orientation to 2050" under Decision No. 1017/QĐ-TTg dated September 21, 2024 of the Prime Minister, candidates must meet the requirements stipulated by MOET Decision 2101/QĐ-BGDĐT of 2025 amending the Training Program Standards for semiconductor microchip programs at undergraduate and master's levels of the Minister of Education; Official Dispatch No. 2101/QĐ-BGDĐT dated July 22, 2025 on amending the Training Program Standards for semiconductor microchip programs at undergraduate and master's levels of the Ministry of Education and Training and in accordance with the 2026 admission regulations of the Ministry of Education and Training.
3.  Foreign language entry requirements apply to all admission methods (except the method based on standardized SAT results).

– The condition ensuring the admission threshold requires a minimum SAT result of ***1100/1600 points.***

English or Japanese foreign language certificates are converted to foreign language scores on the High School Graduation Exam scoring scale and added to the results of the remaining 02 subjects in the admission subject combination to obtain the total admission score.

\+ For the Semiconductor Chip Technology program, the Mathematics score condition for the High School Graduation Exam follows MOET regulations.

***3.2.6. Selection based on interview and candidate evaluation***

*\* Evaluation criteria and admission score thresholds*

– Candidates are admitted if they meet all of the following conditions:

\+ Have the highest preference order for the corresponding training program at Vietnam Japan University and achieve a score equal to or above the cutoff score (after conversion to a 30-point scale) when registering admission preferences on the MOET system.

\+ Candidates holding foreign high school diplomas: Must have a cumulative high school grade point average (GPA) of 2.5 or above and have a clear, lawful personal background as required by law.

\+ Candidates who graduated from high school before 2026 must have a 3-year high school average score for the 02 subjects Mathematics and Literature of 15 points or above.

\+ Candidates taking the 2026 High School Graduation Exam must achieve the quality assurance threshold (minimum score floor) or above as stipulated by VNU.

\+ Meet the foreign language requirements stipulated in Appendix I. The Civil Engineering program does NOT apply foreign language entry requirements.

***10.6.1. Training programs under the Faculty of Interdisciplinary Social Sciences***

The Faculty of Interdisciplinary Social Sciences currently manages 02 undergraduate programs in Japanese Studies and Global Development and Innovation, and 04 Master's programs, 01 Doctoral program, contributing to the mission of training high-quality human resources in the field of interdisciplinary social sciences for sustainable development.

***a) Bachelor's program in Japanese Studies***

The Bachelor's program in Japanese Studies is the first undergraduate training program that Vietnam Japan University chose to develop after considering how to leverage the university's strengths, its ability to contribute to undergraduate education in Vietnam, and to contribute to the development of cooperation between Vietnam and Japan in many aspects in the new era.

Therefore, unlike other traditional Japanese Studies programs, the Japanese Studies program at Vietnam Japan University is a contemporary Japanese studies program: built on an interdisciplinary basis between social sciences and natural sciences and combining social sciences and humanities following the philosophy of liberal arts education and sustainability science, aiming to train Japanese Studies experts who both have comprehensive understanding and master in-depth knowledge in one of three areas: Law and Public Policy, Economics – Management, and Japanese Language Teaching.

Vietnamese bachelor's graduates with understanding of Japan are expected to become experts, managers, and leaders, greatly contributing to promoting comprehensive cooperation between Vietnam and Japan in the coming years. Conversely, the extensive strategic partnership for peace and prosperity in Asia that the two countries are building together is a sustainable foundation for the development of the program.

The advantages of the Japanese Studies program are demonstrated through the following features:

Liberal arts education and sustainability science philosophy: Providing a broad knowledge foundation, including both social sciences and natural sciences, and in-depth knowledge about Japan; Encouraging students to self-study and self-develop to adapt to societal changes in the era of Industrial Revolution 4.0. Valuing the cultural identity of learners.

Contemporaneity: Providing comprehensive knowledge about contemporary Japan as well as current global issues of concern, especially issues related to the sustainable development of humanity.

Opportunity to study in Japan while in Vietnam: In addition to Vietnamese lecturers, the program has cooperation from lecturers from major Japanese universities. Students will participate in many exchange and interaction activities with Japanese students.

Focus on developing foreign language competency: The program is designed with many Japanese language courses and many courses taught in Japanese, so graduates will have a high level of Japanese proficiency, with knowledge cultivated in an international academic environment with the participation of many international lecturers and students.

Modern learning methods: Many courses in the program use active learning methods. Based on the experience of Japanese universities, the programs will incorporate seminar formats into some courses to develop communication skills, discussion, teamwork, and enhance interaction between lecturers and students and among students.

High practicality: The content of courses is highly practical, with the participation of many experienced experts from Japanese and Vietnamese organizations and enterprises. Students will have opportunities to engage with organizations and enterprises through internship, field trip, and exchange programs from the first year.

Opportunity to continue studying in Japan: Students may have opportunities to study at prestigious Japanese universities such as Waseda University, Hosei University, Yokohama University, Tsukuba University... In particular, from 2025, Vietnam Japan University has signed an agreement with Waseda University on dual degree training, where students can transfer to study at Waseda University to obtain 2 graduation degrees from Vietnam Japan University and Waseda University (detailed information about the dual degree program in Section 10.6 below).

***b) Bachelor's program in Global Development and Innovation (International Studies)***

The Bachelor's program in Global Development and Innovation is a new program opened in 2025, aiming to train high-quality human resources with an interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary knowledge foundation on global issues, with systems thinking, critical thinking, innovation capacity, and technology application in analyzing and solving sustainable development challenges in the context of modern international relations; while also having professional ethics, a sense of responsibility, and effective working skills in an international environment, contributing to the sustainable development of Vietnam and the world. The program is taught in English.

The advantages of the Global Development and Innovation program are demonstrated through the following features:

International standard program: Based on world-leading university models, flexibly adjusted to suit Vietnam, the program equips critical thinking, leadership, and innovation to address global challenges that are multicultural and interdisciplinary.

Multicultural, international learning environment: Prestigious lecturers from Japan and many countries; many opportunities for exchange and short-term study at prestigious universities in Japan and other countries.

Links with enterprises and international organizations, expanding career opportunities: Students intern and participate in projects with Japanese and international enterprises, building a solid foundation for their future careers.

Attractive scholarships and financial support: Tuition fees are subsidized, many scholarships for outstanding students, and opportunities for short-term internships in Japan with sponsored costs.

Modern facilities and comprehensive support services: Modern classrooms, laboratories, multilingual libraries, combined with field experience at international organizations and leading enterprises (UNDP, AEON...); academic counseling services, career guidance, dormitories, and diverse extracurricular activities.

Vietnam Japan University and the Protestant University of Applied Sciences Berlin (Evangelische Hochschule Berlin – EHB) officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding on cooperation on December 17, 2025. This event marks an important milestone in VJU's education internationalization strategy for the 2026–2030 period, while opening many opportunities for academic exchange and cultural interaction for students, graduate students, and lecturers of both universities.

Both parties focus on promoting training and research cooperation at the bachelor's level, first within the framework of VJU's Bachelor's program in Global Development and Innovation (BGDI), linked with the Bachelor of Social Work program at EHB. At the same time, gradually expanding cooperation to the master's level, especially programs under VJU's Faculty of Interdisciplinary Social Sciences (FISS), including Global Leadership, Public Policy, Business Administration, and Area Studies.

***10.6.2. Training programs under the Faculty of Advanced Technology and Engineering.***

The Faculty of Advanced Technology and Engineering currently manages 12 training programs, including 05 honors undergraduate programs, 01 standard undergraduate program, 01 combined undergraduate-master's program, 04 master's programs, and plans to open an additional 01 doctoral program in 2026, contributing to the mission of training high-quality human resources in the field of advanced technology and engineering for sustainable development based on leveraging the strengths of Vietnam and Japan.

***a) Bachelor's program in Computer Science and Engineering.***

Unlike other programs in the Computer and Information Science group, the Computer Science and Engineering program at Vietnam Japan University is a liberal arts, comprehensive training program: built on an interdisciplinary basis between computer science and computer engineering, combined with general and essential knowledge for the 21st century, aiming to train high-quality computer science and engineering human resources with competitiveness in the domestic labor market, Japan, and countries around the world in the era of Industrial Revolution 4.0 and Society 5.0.

***b) Engineer's program in Civil Engineering.***

Civil Engineering is the third program that Vietnam Japan University chose to open after considering ways to leverage the university's strengths, its ability to contribute to undergraduate education in Vietnam, and to contribute to the development of cooperation between Vietnam and Japan in many aspects in the new era. The program was opened with technical support from the University of Tokyo, Japan.

Unlike many other programs in the Civil Engineering group, the Civil Engineering program at Vietnam Japan University has been built on an interdisciplinary basis among the specializations of Construction Project Management, Civil and Industrial Construction, Construction Informatics, and Transportation and Urban Engineering. Combined with general and essential knowledge for the 21st century, aiming to train high-quality human resources for domestic and international construction with competitiveness in the domestic labor market, Japan, and countries around the world in the era of Industrial Revolution 4.0 and Society 5.0.

From 2024, Vietnam Japan University adjusted this program to a standard system to restructure the training scale; stratify programs to meet the needs and abilities of learners. The standard program has a smaller course load compared to the honors program and therefore has tuition fees suitable for the majority of learners.

***c) Engineer's program in Smart Agriculture and Sustainability.***

Smart Agriculture and Sustainability is the 4th undergraduate program opened by Vietnam Japan University with technical support from the University of Tokyo, Japan. Agriculture is an important economic sector contributing to the sustainable development of Vietnam and Japan, and is one of the main pillars of cooperation between the two countries. Although it is the origin of Vietnamese culture, plays a key role in national and global food security, this field is facing many challenges regarding climate change, crisis due to shortage of high-quality human resources, and lack of connectivity with advanced science and technology fields in society.

Therefore, the program equips students with a solid foundation of knowledge in agricultural sciences, interdisciplinary knowledge in crop sciences, and helps students possess the ability to apply digital technology to agricultural production chains following 03 specialized orientations in Ecological Agriculture; Advanced Japanese Agriculture applying digital technology 4.0; and Agricultural Enterprise Management to ensure agricultural development in harmony with ecological and humanistic values.

From 2024, the University cooperates with Kochi University, Japan in sending students for annual internships and expanding employment opportunities after graduation.

***d) Combined engineer and master's program in Mechatronics Engineering (Intelligent Mechatronics System & Japanese Manufacturing).***

Intelligent Mechatronics System & Japanese Manufacturing is the fifth program that Vietnam Japan University chose to open after considering the university's strengths, the development potential of the field in undergraduate education in Vietnam, and its contribution to scientific and technical cooperation between Vietnam and Japan in the new era. This is also the first combined engineer and master's program of VJU, built with technical support from Osaka University, Japan.

The program provides learners with knowledge of mathematics and basic sciences to meet the requirements for acquiring field and discipline knowledge about mechatronics system engineering and precision mechanics. In particular, in this program, students are equipped with knowledge of advanced technology in the fields of mechatronics, precision mechanical manufacturing, and materials engineering of Japan. The "Monozukuri" philosophy – Japanese-style manufacturing is thoroughly applied throughout the program, helping students adapt to diverse technological and engineering work environments, and develop Japanese-style scientific production organization and management skills.

***e) Engineer's program in Food Technology and Health***

Food Technology and Health is the sixth program opened by Vietnam Japan University with technical support from the University of Tokyo, Japan. The food and beverage industry in Vietnam is one of the most dynamic and fastest-growing industries. This is an industry with significant contributions to the economy and accounts for a large portion of the country's gross domestic product.

The honors engineer program in Food Technology and Health was opened with the goal of training high-quality human resources for the future food industry. The program provides learners with a solid foundation of knowledge in food science, biology, nutrition, applied biomedical sciences, and strong professional skills in food processing and preservation technology, food safety and health, while also conveying to students a passion for building a safe and sustainable national food industry.

***f) Engineer's program in Semiconductor Chip Technology***

The Semiconductor Chip Technology program was newly opened in 2025 to train high-quality human resources, a comprehensive intellectual generation with a spirit of serving the people, with the capacity to meet the requirements of digital economic development and digital transformation in the new era, the era of Vietnam's national rise;

Training engineers with good political and moral qualities, loyal to the fatherland; with a solid foundation and professional knowledge; with deep expertise in the field of electrical-electronics, semiconductor technology, and integrated circuit design in the context of the digital era and industrialization; with updated knowledge and understanding of semiconductor technology and engineering applied in the semiconductor industry; with the ability to develop science and technology applications commensurate with engineer-level qualifications.

Diversifying training models within Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU). Equipping learners with the ability to effectively apply in-depth theoretical knowledge, professional practice in laboratories, and internships at enterprises and educational institutions in Japan to enhance the internationalization index of VNU's activities.

***g) Bachelor's program in Intelligent Control and Automation***

The Intelligent Control and Automation program was newly opened in 2025 with the general objective of training high-quality human resources with knowledge from basic to modern and practical specialized knowledge in the field of control engineering and automation integrated with artificial intelligence and big data, based on a liberal arts education foundation and lifelong learning capability, with competitiveness in the domestic labor market, globally, and especially in Japan.

– Advantages of the training programs under the Faculty of Advanced Technology and Engineering:

Liberal arts education philosophy: Providing a broad knowledge foundation, including both social sciences and natural sciences, and in-depth knowledge of the training discipline; Encouraging students to self-study and self-develop to adapt to societal changes in the era of Industrial Revolution 4.0 and Society 5.0.

Applicability: Providing students with the ability to develop and create research ideas in interdisciplinary fields and develop application models and solutions in the specialized direction of the training discipline to achieve the objectives of the problem at hand.

Opportunity to study in Japan while in Vietnam: In addition to Vietnamese lecturers, the programs have cooperation from lecturers from major Japanese universities. Students will participate in many exchange and interaction activities with Japanese students.

Focus on developing foreign language competency: The programs are designed with many courses taught in English, so graduates will have an English proficiency equivalent to B2, Level 4 in the 6-level Vietnamese Foreign Language Proficiency Framework. In addition, students learn basic Japanese and can register for advanced Japanese courses as needed.

Modern learning methods: Many courses in the programs use active learning methods. Based on the experience of Japanese universities, the programs will incorporate seminar formats into some courses to develop communication skills, discussion, teamwork, and enhance interaction between lecturers and students and among students.

High practicality: The content of courses is highly practical, with the participation of many experienced experts from Japanese and Vietnamese organizations and enterprises. Students will have opportunities to engage with organizations and enterprises through practical internship programs.

Opportunities to study in Japan: Students may have opportunities to intern, exchange, and study at universities and enterprises in Japan.

**10.7. Dual degree program**

Having affirmed its training quality with Japanese partner organizations, enterprises, and universities, Vietnam Japan University and Waseda University have launched a dual degree program between the two universities. Students participating in this program will study 2 years at Vietnam Japan University and 2 years at Waseda University, Japan. Upon graduation, students receive two degrees from Vietnam Japan University, VNU and Waseda University.

During the last 2 years of study at Waseda University, students only need to pay tuition fees equivalent to those of students studying in Vietnam. Outstanding students will be considered for scholarships during the last 2 years from Waseda University's scholarship sources.

This program is for students with excellent academic performance and good Japanese or English proficiency. Students study at one of the best universities in the world and have the opportunity to experience Japanese culture, one of the richest and most diverse cultures in the world. Living and studying in Japan not only helps students develop soft skills and broaden their vision and understanding of the world, but also provides access to an advanced education system and modern facilities. The multicultural learning environment at Waseda also enables students to interact with and learn from international peers, thereby building a broad and solid network of relationships for the future.

Through the dual degree training agreement, both Vietnam Japan University and Waseda University wish to demonstrate their commitment to promoting academic excellence and nurturing high-quality human resources – serving as a bridge between Vietnam, Japan, and the world.

Vietnam Japan University continues to develop dual degree programs with other partners, especially partners belonging to the Consortium of Japanese Universities cooperating with the University (VJU Consortium).
