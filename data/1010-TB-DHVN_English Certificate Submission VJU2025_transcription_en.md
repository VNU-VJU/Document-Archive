# NOTIFICATION

**Submission of English-language certificates/confirmations for recognition of academic results, credit conversion, and graduation standards for VJU2025 students at Vietnam-Japan University, Vietnam National University, Hanoi**

Pursuant to Decision No. 3626/QĐ-ĐHQGHN dated October 21, 2022 of the Director of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on promulgating the Regulations on undergraduate training at Vietnam National University, Hanoi;
Pursuant to Guideline No. 1011/HD-ĐHQGHN dated March 23, 2023 of the Director of Vietnam National University, Hanoi on organizing foreign language modules in the general knowledge block and determining graduation standards in undergraduate, master's, and doctoral training programs at Vietnam National University, Hanoi;
Pursuant to Guideline No. 880/HD-ĐHVN dated August 11, 2025 of Vietnam-Japan University on guidance for using foreign language certificates to confirm graduation standards for students in the VJU2022 cohort and later;

Vietnam-Japan University hereby notifies the submission of English-language certificates/confirmations for recognition of academic results, credit conversion, and graduation standards for VJU2025 students as follows:

### 1. Requirements for foreign language certificates/confirmations

- Quantity: 01 notarized copy of the certificate/confirmation;
- The certificate/confirmation must remain valid on the submission date.

### 2. For VJU2025 students submitting valid foreign language certificates/confirmations before October 13, 2025

- Submission period: **From 9:00 a.m. on September 15, 2025 to before 4:00 p.m. on October 13, 2025** (9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays);
- Submission location: Room 102, Headquarters Building, Vietnam-Japan University, VNU Urban Area;
- Students whose valid certificates/confirmations meet the requirements of their training program will have their academic results, credit conversions, and English graduation standards recognized.
- Recognition of academic results (on a 10-point scale) is specified as follows:
  - English certificates/confirmations at level 3 correspond to 9 points;
  - English certificates/confirmations at level 4 or higher correspond to 10 points;
- After completing the verification of English certificates/confirmations, Vietnam-Japan University will announce the list of students whose academic results, credit conversions, and graduation standards have been recognized **no later than December 31, 2025**. Students must not register for courses whose results have already been recognized.
- If the verification results are unsuccessful, students must comply with the guidance in Section 2 of this Notification.

### 3. For VJU2025 students submitting foreign language certificates/confirmations after October 13, 2025

- Students who submit certificates/confirmations after October 13, 2025 are only considered for graduation standard recognition; they are not eligible for academic result recognition or credit conversion. In that case, students must register for the English courses in the general knowledge block of the training program.
- Vietnam-Japan University encourages students to submit certificates and confirmations early to ensure verification is completed before graduation consideration.
- Submission location:
  - During the study period at Hoa Lac: Room 102, Headquarters Building, Vietnam-Japan University, VNU Urban Area;
  - During the study period at My Dinh: Room 501, 5th floor, Vietnam-Japan University, Luu Huu Phuoc Street, Nam Tu Liem District, Hanoi.

### 4. Additional notes

- English certificates/confirmations obtained through online examinations are not recognized at Vietnam-Japan University, Vietnam National University, Hanoi;
- The list of English certificates/confirmations is provided in Appendix 1 to this Notification;
- The list of institutions authorized to issue English certificates/confirmations under the Vietnamese six-level foreign language proficiency framework is provided in Appendix 2;
- Students who use falsified certificates/confirmations will be disciplined in accordance with current regulations;
- For inquiries, students should contact Academic Affairs & Student Activities via phongdaotao@st.vju.ac.vn.
